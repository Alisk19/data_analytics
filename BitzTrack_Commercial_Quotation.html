Core Functionalities / Features

1. Analytics & Reporting (Dashboard)
  • KPI Cards: Instant metrics for Total Revenue, Total Orders, Average Order Value, and Expense totals.
  • Dynamic Visualizations: Interactive Line Charts (Revenue/Orders trends), Bar Charts, and Pie Charts (Distribution).
  • Smart Filtering: Time-range data manipulation (Last 7 Days, Last 30 Days) that simultaneously updates all components.
2. Order Management
  • Create new orders with assigned customer data, order values, and expected delivery dates.
  • Modify order statuses manually from the interface.
  • Delete or amend historical order entries.
3. Customer Directory
  • Maintain a digital address book of clients.
  • Automatically aggregated data: view total money spent by each customer and their last activity date.
4. Raw Materials / Inventory Tracking
  • Log materials, quantities, unit metrics, and costs.
  • Monitor stock levels to ensure operational efficiency.
5. System Settings
  • Manage application-wide preferences.
  • Configurable UI/UX settings.
